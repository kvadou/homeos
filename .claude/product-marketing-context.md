# GatherRoot — Product Marketing Context

## Product
**Name:** GatherRoot
**Category:** AI-powered home management platform (SaaS)
**URL:** TBD (domain not yet finalized)

## What It Does
GatherRoot helps homeowners and property managers scan, identify, and manage every item in their home using AI. Users point their phone camera at any appliance, and AI instantly identifies the make, model, and specs. The platform then organizes manuals, warranties, maintenance schedules, and specifications — all searchable and always available. An AI chat lets users ask anything about their home in plain English.

## Target Audiences

### Primary: Homeowners
- Individual homeowners managing 1-3 homes
- Pain points: Lost manuals, expired warranties they don't know about, missed maintenance leading to expensive repairs
- Motivation: Protect their biggest investment, save money, reduce stress
- Language: "I can never find the manual," "I forgot to change the filter," "Is this still under warranty?"

### Secondary: Property Managers
- Managing 3-50+ rental/investment properties
- Pain points: Tracking hundreds of appliances across properties, maintenance coordination, tenant handoffs
- Motivation: Efficiency, reduced maintenance costs, professional organization
- Language: "I need one dashboard for everything," "Which unit needs what serviced?"

## Pricing
- **Free:** $0 forever — 1 home, 25 items, manual entry, basic AI chat
- **Pro:** $9/month ($7/mo annual) — 3 homes, unlimited items, AI scan, full AI chat, maintenance scheduling, warranty tracking
- **Family:** $19/month ($15/mo annual) — Unlimited homes, everything in Pro, multi-user access, provider network, home passport export

## Key Differentiators
1. AI-powered scanning (point camera → instant identification)
2. Automatic manual/spec retrieval (no searching the web)
3. Proactive maintenance reminders (AI predicts what needs attention)
4. Natural language chat about your home ("When was my furnace last serviced?")
5. Provider network for professional help

## Competitors / Alternatives
- Manual spreadsheets / paper filing (most common "competitor")
- Basic home inventory apps (no AI, no maintenance, no chat)
- Home warranty companies (different category, complementary)

## Go-to-Market
- Self-serve (no sales team)
- Freemium model with free-to-paid upgrade
- Target: organic search, content marketing, social proof

## Voice & Tone
- Professional but friendly
- Confident, not hype-y
- Specific over vague ("Save $500/year on missed warranties" not "Save money")
- Direct ("Your home is costing you money right now" not "Consider the potential savings")
- No exclamation points in body copy
