Maintenance Schedule

  1. Replace the air filter every 3 months. Use a 16x25x1 in. pleated filter. A clogged filter is the leading cause of reduced airflow and short cycling.
  2. Inspect the burner assembly annually. Check for soot, corrosion, and proper flame color before each heating season.
  3. Schedule a professional tune-up annually. A licensed HVAC technician should verify gas pressure, heat-exchanger integrity, and combustion safety once per year.
