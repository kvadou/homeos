CORNERSTONE HOME INSPECTIONS

Home Inspection Report — Summary of Findings

Property: 148 Maple Ridge Dr. · Inspection Date: July 10, 2026

Summary of Findings

1. Roof SEVERITY: HIGH
Multiple cracked and missing asphalt shingles observed on the south-facing slope. Active granule loss and two exposed underlayment areas increase the risk of water intrusion.
Recommendation: Have the roof evaluated by a licensed roofer for repair or partial replacement before the next rainy season.
2. Water Heater SEVERITY: MEDIUM
The temperature and pressure relief (TPR) valve lacks a discharge pipe. Discharged hot water could injure an occupant and the condition is a safety code deficiency.
Recommendation: Add a properly routed TPR discharge pipe terminating within 6 inches of the floor.
3. Exterior SEVERITY: LOW
Caulking gaps were noted at several window trim joints on the front elevation. This is routine maintenance, not a structural concern.
Recommendation: Re-caulk the affected window trim to prevent moisture entry.
