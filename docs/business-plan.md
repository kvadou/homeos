# **GatheredOS**

## **High-Level Business Plan**

# **Executive Summary**

## **Mission**

To become the trusted operating system for homeownership by preserving the knowledge of every home and helping families confidently manage, maintain, improve, and protect one of their most important investments.

GatheredOS combines home knowledge, maintenance intelligence, project planning, financial decision support, and AI-powered guidance into a single platform that homeowners rely on throughout the life of their home.

Unlike today's fragmented tools, GatheredOS doesn't simply answer questions—it remembers each home, learns continuously, and helps homeowners make better decisions with confidence.

---

# **Vision**

Every home should have a digital memory that becomes smarter over time.

Just as individuals have financial software to manage their money and businesses have enterprise software to manage operations, every household should have a trusted platform that understands their home, preserves its history, and helps guide every important decision throughout homeownership.

Our long-term vision is for GatheredOS to become the standard operating system that accompanies every home—from the day it is purchased until the day it is sold.

---

# **Problem Statement**

Homeownership is one of the largest financial commitments most people will ever make, yet there is no central system for managing everything that comes with it.

Today, homeowners rely on dozens of disconnected tools:

* Notes apps for maintenance reminders  
* Email folders for warranties and receipts  
* Spreadsheets for renovation budgets  
* Pinterest and Houzz for inspiration  
* Google searches for maintenance advice  
* Zillow for home values  
* Reddit for renovation recommendations  
* Contractor websites for pricing  
* Mortgage calculators for financing  
* Paper manuals stored in drawers  
* Knowledge that exists only in one person's memory

The greatest challenge isn't simply that information is scattered—it's that the knowledge of a home rarely lives with the home itself.

Every house accumulates years of valuable knowledge:

* Which contractor installed the roof  
* When the water heater was replaced  
* Which paint color was used in the guest bedroom  
* Where the water shutoff valve is located  
* Which breaker controls the garage  
* Why a previous repair was made  
* What maintenance has already been completed

When homeowners move, become overwhelmed, forget details, or a family member who managed the home is no longer available, that knowledge is often lost forever.

GatheredOS preserves that knowledge and transforms it into actionable intelligence.

---

# **Why Now?**

Several technology and market trends have converged to make GatheredOS possible.

* AI can now provide personalized guidance at scale.  
* Homeowners are investing record amounts in maintenance and renovations.  
* Smart homes generate more data than ever, but very little context.  
* Homeowners increasingly expect digital tools to manage life's largest investments.  
* Despite advancements in real estate technology, there is still no trusted intelligence layer dedicated to the ongoing lifecycle of homeownership.

The technology now exists to build a platform that simply wasn't possible a few years ago.

# **Core Value Proposition**

GatheredOS helps homeowners answer the questions that matter most:

* What should I prioritize next?  
* Am I taking good care of my home?  
* Is this renovation worth the investment?  
* Can I wait another year before replacing this system?  
* How much should I budget over the next five years?  
* What do I need to remember about this home?  
* What happens if I'm no longer the person managing everything?

Instead of generic advice, GatheredOS provides personalized recommendations based on the homeowner's property, history, goals, finances, location, maintenance records, and accumulated home knowledge.

The platform delivers three core outcomes:

* Greater confidence  
* Better decisions  
* Reduced uncertainty

# **Product Vision**

GatheredOS is built around five connected pillars.

## **1\. Know My Home**

The digital memory of the home.

Includes:

* Systems  
* Appliances  
* Documents  
* Manuals  
* Warranties  
* Paint colors  
* Contractors  
* Photos  
* Measurements  
* Receipts  
* Home Timeline  
* Home Knowledge Vault

This becomes the single source of truth for everything about the property.

---

## **2\. Care For My Home**

AI-powered maintenance intelligence.

Capabilities include:

* Personalized maintenance schedules  
* Seasonal reminders  
* Home Health Score  
* Preventative maintenance recommendations  
* Service history  
* Replacement forecasting  
* Task management  
* Annual maintenance planning

---

## **3\. Improve My Home**

Guides homeowners from idea to completion.

Includes:

* Project evaluation  
* ROI analysis  
* Visual concept generation  
* Budget planning  
* Contractor preparation  
* Material estimates  
* Shopping lists  
* Project timelines  
* Decision tracking

---

## **4\. Protect My Investment**

Provides financial intelligence for every major home decision.

Examples include:

* Estimated project ROI  
* Property value impact  
* Long-term ownership costs  
* Replacement forecasting  
* Opportunity cost  
* Insurance considerations  
* Budget planning  
* Affordability analysis  
* Emergency preparedness

The objective is not simply to calculate costs, but to help homeowners make informed, confident decisions.

---

## **5\. Household Intelligence**

The intelligence layer connecting everything.

Rather than acting as a standalone chatbot, GatheredOS continuously reasons across:

* Home history  
* Maintenance records  
* Systems  
* Projects  
* Documents  
* Budgets  
* Family knowledge  
* Future plans

This allows GatheredOS to provide recommendations that are personalized, contextual, and increasingly valuable over time.

# **Competitive Positioning**

GatheredOS does not replace:

* Real estate websites  
* Contractors  
* Designers  
* Financial advisors  
* Home inspectors

Instead, it becomes the intelligence layer connecting all of them.

Today's alternatives each solve one narrow problem:

* Zillow → Home values  
* Houzz → Inspiration  
* Google → Information  
* Notes apps → Organization  
* Calendar reminders → Maintenance  
* Pinterest → Ideas  
* Contractor marketplaces → Hiring

GatheredOS brings these fragmented experiences together into one persistent system that remembers the home and continuously helps homeowners make better decisions.

Its greatest competitive advantage is the accumulation of knowledge over time.

The longer a household uses GatheredOS, the smarter and more valuable it becomes.
